# Markium — Social Media Warming Plan

"Warming" = building credibility, follower base, and algorithmic trust on each platform **before** launching paid ads or promoting the product hard. You want the accounts to look alive, valuable, and trusted when a new visitor lands on them.

**Duration**: 4-6 weeks of warming before going full promotional.

---

## Channel Priority for Markium

Not every platform is worth the same effort. Rank based on where Algerian merchants actually hang out:

| Rank | Platform | Priority | Why |
|------|----------|----------|-----|
| 1 | **Facebook** | CRITICAL | Where Algerian merchants live. Groups, pages, Marketplace — this is the main channel. |
| 2 | **TikTok** | HIGH | Fast organic reach, young merchants, short-form virality |
| 3 | **Instagram** | HIGH | Visual credibility, Reels cross-post from TikTok, professional brand presence |
| 4 | **YouTube** | MEDIUM | SEO + deep tutorials, long-term discoverability |
| 5 | **LinkedIn** | LOW | Not where Algerian merchants are. Only useful for B2B partnerships (delivery companies, payment providers, investors). Don't waste effort warming it now. |
| 6 | **Twitter/X** | SKIP | Not relevant for Algerian e-commerce audience |

**Bottom line**: Focus 90% effort on Facebook + TikTok + Instagram. YouTube gets 10%. LinkedIn later.

## The Warming Methodology

### Principle 1: Value first, product second
In the warming phase, 80% of content should be **educational and valuable**, 20% brand/product. You're teaching merchants about e-commerce, pixels, profitability, delivery — you're not pitching Markium yet.

### Principle 2: Consistency beats perfection
3 okay posts per week > 1 perfect post per month. Algorithms reward consistency. Set a sustainable pace you can actually maintain.

### Principle 3: Native content per platform
Don't post the same thing everywhere. Repurpose, but adapt to each platform's style:
- **TikTok**: raw, unpolished, fast cuts, trending sounds
- **Instagram Reels**: slightly more polished, captions on screen
- **Facebook**: longer-form, storytelling, emotional hooks
- **YouTube**: deep, educational, well-structured

### Principle 4: Engage, don't just broadcast
Respond to every comment in the first hour (algorithm boost). Follow and interact with other Algerian e-commerce accounts. Build relationships.

### Principle 5: Build in public
Share the journey of building Markium. Behind-the-scenes content performs very well and creates emotional connection.

## Content Pillars (Use across all platforms)

Every piece of content should fit into one of these 5 buckets:

### Pillar 1: Education (40%)
Teach merchants something useful about e-commerce in Algeria.
- "How Facebook Pixel really works"
- "What is CAPI and why Meta keeps warning you"
- "How to calculate your real profit margin"
- "Why your return rate is high and how to fix it"
- "Best times to post on Facebook in Algeria"

### Pillar 2: Insight / Data (20%)
Share stats, benchmarks, or observations that position Markium as the expert.
- "Average conversion rate in Algerian e-commerce: 1.8%"
- "Wilayas with highest return rates"
- "Most profitable product categories in 2026"
- "We analyzed 10,000 orders — here's what we found"

### Pillar 3: Story / Case Study (15%)
Real merchants, real numbers, real stories. (Start with fictional/anonymized stories if you don't have merchants yet.)
- "How [merchant] went from 4% margin to 22%"
- "The merchant who discovered 40% of her ad spend was wasted"
- "From spreadsheets to dashboards: one merchant's 2-hour/day win"

### Pillar 4: Behind the Scenes (15%)
Build-in-public content. Show the team, the product evolution, the mission.
- Design sneak peeks
- "Why we built Markium in Darija"
- Team introductions
- Product roadmap teases

### Pillar 5: Community / Engagement (10%)
Polls, questions, responses to community pain points.
- "What's your biggest challenge running your store?"
- "Which feature should we build next?"
- "Tag a merchant who needs to see this"
- Response videos to comments

## Per-Platform Playbook

### FACEBOOK (Priority 1)

**Why critical**: This is where Algerian merchants already are. Groups, pages, marketplace activity — the entire ecosystem.

**Setup:**
- Create **Facebook Page** (Markium) — optimized bio, profile photo, cover image
- Create **Facebook Group** (مجتمع ماركيوم / Markium Community) — this is the long game
- Join the top 30 Algerian e-commerce groups as Markium Page — be a helpful member

**Content format:**
- **Reels** (30-60s) — 2x/week — educational, same as TikTok
- **Carousels** (5-10 slides) — 2x/week — step-by-step tutorials, data insights
- **Static posts** with strong copy — 1x/week — quotes, stats, questions
- **Stories** — daily — behind-the-scenes, polls, quick tips
- **Long-form text posts** — 1x/week — storytelling, building emotional connection

**Schedule (first 4 weeks):**
| Day | Content Type |
|-----|-------------|
| Mon | Educational carousel |
| Tue | Data insight post |
| Wed | Reel (tutorial) |
| Thu | Behind-the-scenes / BIP post |
| Fri | Reel (story/case study) |
| Sat | Community question |
| Sun | Rest or story recap |

**Optimal posting times** (Algeria):
- Morning: 8-10 AM
- Evening peak: **8-11 PM** (best window)
- Avoid: prayer times (especially Friday midday)

**Engagement tactics:**
- Spend 30 minutes/day commenting on posts in Algerian e-commerce groups
- Answer questions genuinely, drop Markium only when relevant
- Follow-and-engage with top Algerian e-commerce creators daily

### TIKTOK (Priority 2)

**Why important**: Algorithmic reach is incredible — a single video can reach 100K+ Algerian viewers with zero follower base. Best platform for rapid brand awareness.

**Setup:**
- Create account `@markium` or `@markium_dz`
- Bio: clear value prop in Darija + link
- Profile photo: simple logo mark

**Content format:**
- **Short tutorials** (15-30s) — screen recordings + voice
- **Talking head** (30-60s) — face-to-camera tips
- **Before/after** — spreadsheet vs Markium dashboard
- **Trend-jacking** — use trending sounds with e-commerce angle
- **POV videos** — "POV: you just discovered your ad campaign is losing money"

**Schedule:**
- **Posting frequency**: 5-7 posts per week during warming phase
- Yes, this is aggressive. TikTok rewards volume. If you can't sustain 5/week, do 4.
- **Optimal times** (Algeria): 7-9 PM, 11 PM-1 AM

**Warming tactics:**
1. First 10 posts: purely educational, no product mention
2. Post 11-20: start mentioning Markium in passing ("I use a tool called Markium for this")
3. Post 21+: proper promotional mix
4. **Engage on other creators' videos** — comment thoughtfully on Algerian e-commerce content. TikTok's algorithm rewards active accounts.
5. **Reply to comments with video responses** — this is a major TikTok hack that boosts reach

**Viral format to try:**
- "3 things Algerian merchants do wrong"
- "I analyzed 100 Algerian online stores — here's what I found"
- "Your Facebook ads are broken. Here's why."
- "Stop using Excel. Watch this."

### YOUTUBE (Priority 4)

**Why medium priority**: Long-term asset. SEO value. Deep tutorials build authority. Not great for warming (slow to gain traction) but worth seeding content now.

**Setup:**
- Create channel "Markium" with clean branding
- Channel trailer (60s) explaining what Markium is
- Organize into playlists: Tutorials, Features, E-commerce Tips

**Content format:**
- **Long tutorials** (5-15 min) — deep dive into features
- **Shorts** (under 60s) — repurpose TikTok content
- **Webinar recordings** — once you start doing webinars

**Schedule:**
- **1 long video per week** during warming phase (realistic)
- **3-5 Shorts per week** (easy — repurpose from TikTok)

**Topics for long videos:**
1. "Complete Markium Setup Tutorial" (10 min)
2. "How to Set Up Facebook Pixel + CAPI in 5 Minutes" (5 min)
3. "Understanding Your E-commerce Profit Margin" (8 min)
4. "How to Read Your Analytics Dashboard" (10 min)

**SEO optimization:**
- Titles in French and Arabic (reach bilingual audience)
- Descriptions with keywords
- Closed captions in Darija + French
- Custom thumbnails with Darija text

## 6-Week Warming Schedule (Week-by-Week)

### Week 1: Setup & Baseline
- [ ] Create all accounts (FB page, FB group, TikTok, IG, YouTube)
- [ ] Optimize profiles (bios, photos, links)
- [ ] Post 1 introduction post per platform
- [ ] Join top 30 FB e-commerce groups
- [ ] Follow 100 Algerian e-commerce creators
- [ ] Create content calendar template
- **Posts this week**: 3-5 total across platforms (just getting started)

### Week 2: Find Your Voice
- [ ] 3 TikTok videos (test different formats)
- [ ] 2 Facebook Reels
- [ ] 2 IG Reels
- [ ] 1 Facebook carousel (educational)
- [ ] Daily stories on FB + IG
- [ ] 30 min/day engaging on competitor/community content
- **Posts this week**: 8-10 total

### Week 3: Ramp Up
- [ ] 5 TikTok videos
- [ ] 3 Facebook Reels
- [ ] 3 IG Reels
- [ ] 2 Facebook carousels
- [ ] 1 long YouTube video (Complete Setup Tutorial)
- [ ] First Facebook Group post (in your own group)
- **Posts this week**: 12-15 total

### Week 4: Consistency Lock-In
- Same volume as Week 3
- Start tracking which content types perform best
- **Double down on winners**, drop losers
- Start collecting emails/WhatsApp numbers from engaged followers

### Week 5: Introduce Product More Assertively
- Shift from 80/20 value/brand → 70/30
- Start including more Markium screenshots in content
- First "soft pitch" videos ("I built this tool called Markium")
- Host a Facebook Live Q&A

### Week 6: Ready for Launch
- Content library of 40-60 pieces across platforms
- Followers: realistic target 500-2000 per platform
- Engaged community forming in Facebook group
- Ready to layer in **paid ads** on top of warm organic presence

## Critical Warming Rules

1. **Never ask for sales during warming.** No "sign up now" CTAs. Only "follow for more tips" or "comment your question."
2. **Never use stock-looking content.** Raw beats polished in this market.
3. **Post in Darija as your default**, French second, formal Arabic almost never.
4. **Respond to every comment within 1 hour when possible.** First-hour engagement is the biggest algorithm signal.
5. **Build the Facebook group deliberately.** It's your highest-value long-term asset. Post daily even if only you and 5 members are there.
6. **Batch, don't hustle.** Sunday = content production day. The rest of the week = scheduling and engaging.
7. **Track what works, double down.** After week 2, you'll know which format + topic combos resonate. Stop wasting time on the rest.
