# Markium — Marketing Video Angles

Each video targets a different pain point or desire. All videos should be in Darija, 30-60 seconds, and end with the same CTA: "جرب Markium مجانا" (Try Markium for free).

---

## Angle 1: "The Blind Merchant" — You Don't Know Your Real Profit

**Pain**: Merchants think they're making money because they see sales, but they've never calculated actual profit after costs.

**Hook**: "تبيع 100 commande في اليوم وتحسب روحك تربح... بصح واش حسبت les frais de livraison؟ التغليف؟ الإعلانات؟"
*(You sell 100 orders/day and think you're profiting... but did you count delivery fees? Packaging? Ads?)*

**Story**: Show a merchant celebrating sales numbers → then show the costs piling up → his face drops → Markium P&L dashboard reveals real margin is 4% → he adjusts prices and cuts a bad campaign → margin jumps to 22%.

**Key feature shown**: Revenue Stream / Profitability dashboard

**Emotion**: Shock → awareness → relief

## Angle 2: "The Wasted Ad Budget" — Your Ads Are Burning Money

**Pain**: Merchants spend heavily on Facebook/TikTok ads with no way to know which campaigns actually generate profit.

**Key feature shown**: Campaign ROI + Channel breakdown

**Emotion**: Fear of loss → clarity → smart decision

## Angle 4: "The Ghost Customer" — Did Your Customer Even See The Tracking?

**Pain**: Merchants send tracking links but have no idea if customers actually check them. Customers who don't check often refuse delivery.

**Hook**: "بعثت الترقب للزبون وما فتحوش. نهار التوصيل؟ رفض الطرد. خسرت frais de livraison على الفارغ."
*(You sent tracking to the customer and they never opened it. Delivery day? They refused the package. You lost delivery fees for nothing.)*

**Story**: Merchant sends WhatsApp tracking link → Markium shows "0 views" on the tracking analytics card → merchant calls the customer proactively → customer confirms the order → successful delivery. Contrast with: another order where tracking was viewed 5 times = guaranteed delivery.

**Key feature shown**: Order tracking analytics (view count, last view status)

**Emotion**: Loss prevention → proactive action → saved money

## Video Production Notes

### Format
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds for TikTok/Reels, 2-3 minutes for YouTube
- **Language**: 100% Darija (Algerian Arabic), subtitles in French
- **Style**: Raw, authentic, not corporate — screen recordings + face-to-camera
- **Music**: Trending sounds on TikTok, subtle background on Facebook

### Structure for each video
1. **Hook** (0-3s): Strong opening line that calls out the pain
2. **Problem** (3-10s): Show the frustration visually
3. **Solution** (10-25s): Markium dashboard doing its thing
4. **Result** (25-35s): The outcome — more profit, saved time, fewer returns
5. **CTA** (35-40s): "جرب Markium مجانا — الرابط في البيو" (Try Markium free — link in bio)

### Testing strategy
- Launch 3-4 angles first as organic posts
- Measure which gets most engagement (saves, shares, comments)
- Turn top 2 performers into paid ads
- Iterate: reshoot winning angles with better production
