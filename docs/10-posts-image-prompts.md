Use these prompts in **ChatGPT (DALL-E)**, **Midjourney**, or **Canva Magic Media**.

**Brand specs to keep consistent:**
- Colors: Deep blue (#1A2E5A), Electric green (#00D67E), White
- Style: Clean, modern, minimal, professional
- Size: 1080x1080 (Instagram/Facebook), 1080x1920 (TikTok/Stories/Reels cover)
- Text: Add text manually in Canva AFTER generating the background — AI handles text poorly in Arabic

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Generate 3-4 variations** per prompt and pick the best one
2. **Never rely on AI for Arabic text** — it will be gibberish. Always add text manually in Canva
3. **Keep brand consistency**: same dark blue (#1A2E5A) + green (#00D67E) across all posts
4. **Use Canva brand kit**: upload Markium logo, set brand colors and fonts once, apply everywhere
5. **For carousels**: generate each slide separately, then assemble in Canva with consistent template
6. **If DALL-E gives weird results**: simplify the prompt. Remove adjectives. Focus on one main visual idea.
7. **Midjourney tip**: add `--ar 1:1` for square, `--ar 9:16` for stories/TikTok
8. **Save winners as templates**: if a style works, describe it in future prompts as "same style as [description]"
